Vegan cherry Pie

PASTRY FILLING
flour (375g) 🍒 (350g)
flora plant (250g) vanilla extract
sugar (150g) sugar (2 tbs)
salt (pinch)

1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Mix flora plant, sugar and vanilla until light and fluffy. Add in flour and salt.
2. Mix flora plant and sugar until fluffy. Add dry ingredients.
3. Press ¾ of mixture into pie dish. Bake 20-25 minutes until golden.
4. In the meantime, pit and halve the cherries. Add in vanilla and sugar.
5. Remove crust from oven and add the cherries. Sprinkle or press remaining dough on top.
